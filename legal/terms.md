## 1. Nature of the service

AgriFlow is **informational middleware**. We publish public listings, indicative prices,
voluntary provenance "preparation aids", and warm-introduction connections.

**AgriFlow does not, in any case:**

- Hold inventory or take legal title to goods
- Process payments, escrow funds, or act as a payment service provider
- Set prices, payment terms, return windows, or contractual obligations between users
- Act as agent, broker, or intermediary for any commercial transaction
- Issue Due Diligence Statements under Regulation (EU) 2023/1115 (EUDR) — our
  PostcardSSL artefacts are voluntary preparation aids, never DDSs

All commercial transactions occur **directly** between producer and buyer. AgriFlow has
no role in, liability for, or warranty over any sale, exchange, delivery, or contract
arising from a referral introduced through the platform.

You must be 18+, established within the European Economic Area or a recognised trade
